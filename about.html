About Us

Flowers For You

Welcome to Floral Delights, where floral dreams come to life. We are a passionate team of florists dedicated to
spreading beauty, love, and joy through the language of flowers.

Floral Delights was founded in 2023 by Rachelle Pantinople, a floral enthusiast with a vision to create a place
where the vibrant colors and fragrant scents of fresh blooms could brighten people's lives. What started as
a small, local flower shop has grown into a beloved community institution known for its exquisite floral
arrangements and exceptional customer service.

At Floral Delights, we are committed to providing our customers with the finest flowers and arrangements
that nature has to offer. Every bouquet we create is a masterpiece, handcrafted with care and attention
to detail. We source our blooms from local and global growers who share our commitment to quality and
sustainability.

From stunning bridal bouquets to elegant centerpieces and sympathy arrangements, our floral expertise
knows no bounds. We offer a wide range of services, including:
Everyday Floral Arrangements
Wedding and Event Florals
Sympathy and Funeral Flowers
Corporate Floral Services
Seasonal and Holiday Decor
